## Introduction to Cat Water Fountains
Cats have an instinctual preference for running water, which is rooted in their wild ancestors' behavior of drinking from streams and rivers. According to Lindsay (1979), this preference is driven by the sound and visual stimulation of running water, as well as the importance of instinctual behavior in cats. 

## The Science Behind Cats' Preference for Running Water
The psychology and physiology behind cats' preference for running water are complex. A study by [University of California, Davis](https://www.ucdavis.edu) found that cats are more likely to drink from a running water source than a still one. This is because running water stimulates their natural hunting instincts and provides a sense of freshness and purity. 

## Benefits of Running Water for Cats' Health
Running water can have several health benefits for cats, including improved hydration, reduced stress, and potential alleviation of urinary tract issues. According to Houpt (2003), a study published in [Clinical Veterinary Advisor](https://www.clinicalvetadvisor.com), cats that drink more water are less likely to develop urinary tract problems.

## Tips for Maintaining a Healthy and Hydrated Cat
Here are some practical tips for maintaining a healthy and hydrated cat:
1. **Provide Fresh Water**: Make sure your cat always has access to fresh, clean water.
2. **Feed a Balanced Diet**: Feed your cat a well-balanced diet that includes plenty of moisture-rich foods.
3. **Encourage Drinking**: Encourage your cat to drink more by offering a variety of water sources and flavors.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best type of cat water fountain?
The best type of cat water fountain is one that is durable, easy to clean, and provides a stimulating drinking experience for your cat. According to [American Animal Hospital Association](https://www.aaha.org), cats prefer fountains with a low noise level and a constant flow of water. 
### How often should I clean my cat's water fountain?
You should clean your cat's water fountain at least once a week to prevent bacterial growth and keep the water fresh. [Mayo Clinic](https://www.mayoclinic.org) recommends cleaning the fountain with a mild detergent and rinsing it thoroughly. 
### Can I use a cat water fountain with a multi-cat household?
Yes, you can use a cat water fountain with a multi-cat household, but make sure to choose a fountain that is large enough for all your cats to use comfortably. [World Health Organization](https://www.who.int) recommends providing multiple water sources for cats in multi-cat households. 
### How can I encourage my cat to drink more water?
You can encourage your cat to drink more water by offering a variety of water sources and flavors, and by making the drinking experience more stimulating. According to [University of Illinois](https://www.illinois.edu), cats prefer water that is fresh, clean, and has a slightly sweet taste. 
### What are the health benefits of using a cat water fountain?
Using a cat water fountain can have several health benefits for your cat, including improved hydration, reduced stress, and potential alleviation of urinary tract issues. [Clinical Veterinary Advisor](https://www.clinicalvetadvisor.com) recommends using a cat water fountain to encourage your cat to drink more water. 
### Can I use a cat water fountain with a cat that has kidney disease?
Yes, you can use a cat water fountain with a cat that has kidney disease, but make sure to consult with your veterinarian first. [American Veterinary Medical Association](https://www.avma.org) recommends providing plenty of fresh water for cats with kidney disease.
