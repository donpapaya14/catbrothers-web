When it comes to traveling with your feline friend, choosing the right **airline approved cat carriers** is crucial. ## The 3 TSA Features Most Carriers Lack
To speed up security checks, look for carriers with the following features:
1. Removable top panels
2. Steel-reinforced zippers
3. Quick-release straps
These features are recommended by the [TSA Pet Screening Guidelines 2023](https://www.tsa.gov/travel/special-procedures/traveling-with-pets).

## How to Measure Your Cat Correctly
Measuring your cat correctly is crucial to ensure a comfortable and safe flight. The [IATA Live Animal Regulations](https://www.iata.org/en/publications/Pages/live-animals.aspx) provide a diagram showing the correct way to measure your cat:
* Nose-to-rump: Measure from the tip of your cat's nose to the base of their tail
* Curled position: Measure your cat while they are curled up, as this will give you the most accurate measurement
For long-haired breeds, it's essential to take into account their fur length when measuring.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best cat carrier for flying?
The best cat carrier for flying is one that is **airline approved** and meets the specific requirements of your airline. Look for carriers with removable top panels, steel-reinforced zippers, and quick-release straps.
### How do I measure my cat for a carrier?
To measure your cat, use the nose-to-rump and curled position methods, taking into account their fur length if they are a long-haired breed.
### Can I use a hard-shell carrier on any airline?
No, not all airlines allow hard-shell carriers. Check with your airline before booking your flight to ensure you have the correct type of carrier.
### What is the maximum size of a cat carrier for under-seat storage?
The maximum size of a cat carrier for under-seat storage varies by airline, but most have a maximum width of 18-19 inches and a maximum height of 11-13 inches.
### How can I reduce my cat's stress during layovers?
To reduce your cat's stress during layovers, provide a comfortable and stress-free environment, avoid using carrier-mounted food and water bowls, and opt for a spill-proof design instead.

The most important feature of a cat carrier for air travel is that it is **TSA compliant** and meets the specific requirements of your airline.
