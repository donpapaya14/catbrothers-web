## The Difference Between Whole Salmon and Fish Meal
According to the [National Research Council (NRC) 2006 publication](https://www.nap.edu) on nutrient requirements of cats, whole salmon provides more nutrients than fish meal. **Whole salmon** contains **40% protein**, while fish meal contains **25% protein**. However, fish meal can be a more affordable and sustainable option.

## Debunking the Myth: Does Grain-Free Mean Higher Salmon Content?
A study by the [Journal of Animal Science (2018)](https://academic.oup.com/jas) found that grain-free diets do not necessarily mean higher **salmon protein content**. In fact, some grain-free cat foods may contain lower levels of protein due to the use of alternative ingredients.
