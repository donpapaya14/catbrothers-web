## Introduction to Best Litter Boxes for Multi-Cat Households
For **multi-cat households**, finding the right litter box is crucial for maintaining cleanliness and reducing stress among cats. According to the [ASPCA](https://www.aspca.org), the ideal litter box size for a multi-cat household is at least **1.5 times the length** of the cat. 

## Litter Box Size and Capacity
The **ASPCA's Litter Box Guidelines (2020)** recommend that each cat have its own litter box, with a minimum capacity of **2-3 inches of litter depth**. A larger litter box with adequate capacity is essential for multi-cat households to prevent accidents and reduce odor.

## Litter Box Odor Control and Maintenance
According to [Cornell University's College of Veterinary Medicine (2019)](https://www.vet.cornell.edu), regular cleaning and maintenance of litter boxes can help reduce odor. Here are some tips:
1. Scoop out solid waste daily
2. Change litter completely every 7-10 days
3. Use odor control systems or litter box liners

## Common Litter Box Issues in Multi-Cat Households
Multi-cat households often face issues such as **territorial marking**, **stress**, and **accidents outside of the litter box**. A study by the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery (2018)](https://www.jfms.com) found that **40% of multi-cat households** experience litter box issues.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best litter box for multi-cat households?
The best litter box for multi-cat households is one that is large enough to accommodate all cats, with a minimum capacity of **2-3 inches of litter depth**. Consider the IRIS USA Large Capacity Litter Box or the Nature's Miracle Advanced Odor-Control Litter Box.
### How often should I clean the litter box?
Clean the litter box daily by scooping out solid waste, and change the litter completely every 7-10 days.
### What are the common litter box issues in multi-cat households?
Common litter box issues in multi-cat households include **territorial marking**, **stress**, and **accidents outside of the litter box**.
### How can I reduce odor in the litter box?
Reduce odor in the litter box by using odor control systems or litter box liners, and by cleaning the litter box regularly.
### What is the best litter for multi-cat households?
The best litter for multi-cat households is one that is **odor-controlling** and **absorbent**. Consider using clumping litter or silica gel litter.
### Can I use a DIY litter box solution?
Yes, you can use a DIY litter box solution using plastic containers and litter. However, ensure that the container is large enough to accommodate all cats and has adequate ventilation.
