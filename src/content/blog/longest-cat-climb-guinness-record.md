## Meet the Record-Breaking Cat: Lil Bub's Cousin
Lil Nick, a talented feline, holds the Guinness World Record for the **longest cat climb**, according to Guinness World Records, 2020. Owned by Alex Hogan, Lil Nick has shown remarkable agility and climbing skills.

## The Record-Breaking Climb: 102.2 Feet!
On a custom-built climbing wall, Lil Nick achieved a **102.2 feet** climb, overcoming various obstacles, as reported by Guinness World Records, 2020.

## The Science Behind Cat Climbing: Muscles and Flexibility
Cats have **flexor muscles** in their legs, allowing them to climb, as explained in the Journal of Anatomy, 2018. Their **righting reflex** also helps them to always land on their feet.

## Creating a Safe and Fun Climbing Environment
To create a safe climbing environment, follow these tips:
* **Secure the climbing wall**: Ensure it is stable and won't topple over.
* **Provide a soft landing**: Place a mattress or padding at the base of the climbing wall.
As recommended by the American Society for the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (ASPCA), 2020, always supervise your cat during climbing activities.

## The Benefits of Cat Climbing: Exercise and Mental Stimulation
Climbing provides **exercise** and **mental stimulation** for cats, improving their overall health and well-being, according to Veterinary Clinics of North America: Small Animal Practice, 2019.
