### What are the benefits of jumping for cats?
Jumping is an essential form of exercise for cats, as it helps to **improve their flexibility**, **balance**, and **overall health**. According to a study published in the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/journal-of-feline-medicine-and-surgery) in 2018, regular exercise can help to reduce the risk of obesity and other health problems in cats.
### Can cats jump too high?
Yes, cats can jump too high, which can lead to **injuries**. It's essential to provide a safe and stable environment for your cat to jump and play, and to supervise them during playtime to ensure their safety.
### How can I prevent my cat from getting injured while jumping?
You can prevent your cat from getting injured while jumping by providing a safe and stable environment, complete with **soft landing surfaces** and **stable structures**. You can also try to **limit the height** of your cat's jumps by placing toys or treats on lower shelves.
