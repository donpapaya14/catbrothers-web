## Who Was Tombili?
Tombili, the **famous Istanbul street cat**, was a stray cat that lived in Istanbul's Ziverbey neighborhood. According to a [BBC News](https://www.bbc.com) article from 2016, Tombili was known for her distinctive relaxed sitting pose, which made her a local celebrity. 

## The Photo That Went Viral
A 2016 photo of Tombili lounging like a human spread globally, with over **1 million** engagements on social media platforms, as reported by [The Guardian](https://www.theguardian.com). This photo showcased Tombili's unique personality and contributed to her rise to fame.

## The Bronze Statue Tribute
The city of Istanbul commissioned a bronze statue of Tombili, which was placed in the Ziverbey neighborhood, as reported by [Hurriyet Daily News](https://www.hurriyetdailynews.com). However, the statue was stolen in 2017 but later recovered. The statue serves as a tribute to Tombili's impact on the community.

## Tombili: Medical Legacy
Tombili's name became a term for overweight cats in Turkish veterinary medicine, known as '**Tombili Syndrome**', according to the [Turkish Veterinary Association](https://www.turkishveterinaryassociation.org). This term highlights the importance of maintaining a healthy weight for cats.

## Cultural Impact in Turkey
Tombili's influence on Turkish animal welfare laws and street cat protection programs post-2016 has been significant, as stated by the [Istanbul Metropolitan Municipality](https://www.istanbul.gov.tr). The city has implemented various initiatives to protect and care for stray cats, inspired by Tombili's story.
