## Why Senior Cats Prefer Running Water
A study by the [Cornell University College of Veterinary Medicine](https://www.vet.cornell.edu) (2019) found that **70% of cats** prefer running water to still water. This is because running water stimulates their natural hunting instinct and encourages them to drink more. The study also highlighted the importance of **water flow rates** and **noise levels** in cat water fountains, as these can affect a cat's willingness to drink from the fountain.

## Tips for Introducing a New Water Fountain to Your Senior Cat
Introducing a new water fountain to your senior cat can be a challenging task. Here are some tips to follow:
1. Place the fountain in a quiet and accessible location.
2. Start with a low water flow rate and gradually increase it as your cat becomes accustomed to the fountain.
3. Reward your cat for using the fountain with treats and praise.
According to expert advice from the [ASPCA](https://www.aspca.org), it's essential to introduce the fountain gradually and make it a positive experience for your cat.
