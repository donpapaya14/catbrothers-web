## How Daylight Saving Affects Cat Sleep
The time change associated with **daylight saving** can significantly impact **cat sleep patterns**, with a **30% increase in sleep deprivation** for the first few days, according to the [National Sleep Foundation](https://www.sleepfoundation.org), 2020. This disruption can lead to changes in your cat's behavior and overall well-being.

## Tips to Help Your Cat Adjust
To minimize the disruption, establish a consistent **sleep schedule** and create a cozy **sleep environment**. According to the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery](https://www.jfms.com), 2018, providing a quiet and comfortable space can help your cat adjust to the new sleep schedule. Here are some practical tips:
1. Gradually adjust your cat's sleep schedule a few days before the time change.
2. Ensure your cat's sleep environment is dark, quiet, and at a comfortable temperature.
3. Avoid stimulating activities before bedtime.

## Cat Sleep Patterns Before and After Daylight Saving
A comparison of **cat sleep patterns** before and after **daylight saving** reveals significant changes in sleep duration and quality. According to the [Sleep Research Society](https://www.sleepresearchsociety.org), 2015, cats sleep for an average of **16-18 hours** per day. After the time change, cats may experience a decrease in sleep quality and duration.
| Sleep Pattern | Before Daylight Saving | After Daylight Saving |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sleep Duration | 16-18 hours | 14-16 hours |
| Sleep Quality | High | Low |

## Common Cat Behaviors During Daylight Saving
During the time change, cats may exhibit **increased anxiety and restlessness**, according to the [American Animal Hospital Association](https://www.aaha.org), 2020. Other common behaviors include:
* Increased vocalization
* Changes in appetite
* Increased activity levels

## How to Support Your Cat's Sleep Health
To support your cat's **sleep health**, create a sleep-conducive environment and avoid stimulating activities before bedtime. According to the [Feline Sleep Health Initiative](https://www.felinehealth.org), 2019, providing a comfortable and quiet space can help improve your cat's sleep quality. Here are some tips:
1. Provide a comfortable and supportive bed.
2. Ensure the sleep environment is dark and quiet.
3. Avoid stimulating activities before bedtime.

## Conclusion: Minimizing the Impact of Daylight Saving on Your Cat's Sleep
To minimize the impact of **daylight saving** on your cat's sleep, establish a consistent sleep schedule and create a cozy sleep environment. By following these tips, you can help your cat adjust to the new sleep schedule and improve their overall well-being.
