## Introduction to Cat Behavior
Cats knocking things off tables is a common phenomenon that has puzzled many cat owners. According to a study by Kiddie et al. ## Preventing Table-Surfing Behavior
To prevent cats from knocking things off tables, it's essential to provide alternative surfaces and increase exercise and play. A study by Mills (2018) published in the Journal of Feline Behavior found that cats that engage in regular play and exercise are less likely to exhibit destructive behavior. 

1. Provide a cat tower or scratching post near the table
2. Increase playtime to at least 30 minutes per day
3. Use deterrents such as double-sided tape or aluminum foil to discourage jumping

## Conclusion
