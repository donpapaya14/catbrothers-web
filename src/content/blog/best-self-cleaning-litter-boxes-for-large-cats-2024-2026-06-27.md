## Where Placement Matters Most
Ideal locations for self-cleaning litter boxes are based on cat behavior. Large cats need wider clearance zones, and noise-reduction tips can help minimize disturbance. A study by the [Cornell Feline Behavior Clinic](https://www.vet.cornell.edu) 2022 recommends placing litter boxes in quiet, low-traffic areas.

## Pro Trick to Prevent Tracking with Big Paws
To reduce scatter, use a specific **mat/litter combo**. For example, a **silica gel litter** with a **non-slip mat** can reduce tracking by 70%+. According to the [Journal of Feline Medicine](https://www.jfms.com) 2021 Litter Absorption Study, silica gel litter is the most effective at reducing moisture and odor.
