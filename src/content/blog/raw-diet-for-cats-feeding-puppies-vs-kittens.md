## Introduction to Raw Diet for Kittens vs Puppies
When it comes to deciding on a **raw diet for kittens vs puppies**, it's essential to understand the different nutritional requirements of each species. According to the [National Research Council](https://www.nap.edu), kittens require a diet rich in **taurine** and **vitamin A**, while puppies require a diet rich in **protein** and **calcium**.

## Nutritional Requirements for Kittens vs Puppies
The nutritional requirements for kittens and puppies differ significantly. Kittens require a diet that is **high in protein** (around 30% on a dry matter basis) and **fat** (around 20% on a dry matter basis), while puppies require a diet that is **high in protein** (around 25% on a dry matter basis) and **complex carbohydrates**. A study by the [National Research Council (2011)](https://www.nap.edu) found that kittens require a minimum of **0.2% taurine** in their diet, while puppies require a minimum of **1.0% calcium**.

## Risks of Feeding Raw to Kittens vs Puppies
Feeding raw to kittens and puppies can pose significant health risks if not done properly. According to the [Association of American Feed Control Officials (2019)](https://www.aafco.org), raw diets can be contaminated with **bacteria** such as **Salmonella** and **E. coli**, which can cause serious illness in both kittens and puppies. A study by the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery (2018)](https://journals.sagepub.com/home/jfm) found that **25% of raw meat samples** were contaminated with **bacteria**.
