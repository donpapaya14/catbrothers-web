## When to Avoid Self-Cleaning Boxes
While self-cleaning litter boxes are convenient, there are scenarios where they might not be the best choice:
- **Multi-cat households**: May require more frequent cleaning.
- **Senior cats**: Might prefer traditional litter boxes due to familiarity.
- **Power outage preparedness**: Consider backup options for litter box functionality during outages.

According to the [Tufts University Feline Behavior Clinic](https://www.tufts.edu), understanding these limitations is crucial for ensuring the well-being of your cat.
