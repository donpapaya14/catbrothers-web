## The Amazing Capacity of Cat Smell
Cats use their sense of smell to detect pheromones, which are chemical signals that convey information between members of the same species. A study published in [Animal Behaviour](https://www.sciencedirect.com/journal/animal-behaviour), 2012, found that cats use pheromones to mark their territory and signal their reproductive status.

## Cat Smell: The Key to Tracking Prey
Cats use their sense of smell to track prey, including small rodents. According to a study published in the [Journal of Comparative Physiology A](https://link.springer.com/journal/355), 2015, cats can detect the scent of prey and follow their tracks. Here are some tips to help your cat use its sense of smell to track prey:
1. Provide a **stimulating environment** with plenty of hiding spots and toys.
2. Use **pheromone diffusers** to mimic the scent of prey.
3. Play **scent games** with your cat, such as hiding treats or toys with a strong scent.
