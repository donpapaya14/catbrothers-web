## Safety Precautions for High-Jumping Cats
When encouraging your cat to jump high, it's essential to take safety precautions to prevent **injuries**. Here are some tips:
1. **Secure the area**: Ensure that the jumping area is clear of any **hazards** or **obstacles** that could cause your cat to fall or get hurt.
2. **Provide a soft landing**: Place a **soft surface**, such as a mattress or a thick blanket, under the jumping area to cushion your cat's landing.
3. **Supervise your cat**: Always supervise your cat when they are jumping to ensure their safety and provide assistance if needed.
