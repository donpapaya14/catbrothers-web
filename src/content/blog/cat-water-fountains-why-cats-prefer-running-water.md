## Benefits of Running Water for Your Cat's Health
Running water can have several benefits for your cat's health, including improved oral health and increased hydration. According to a study by the **American Veterinary Dental Society** (2018), running water can help reduce tartar buildup and improve gum health in cats. ## Tips for Choosing the Right Cat Water Fountain for Your Home
When choosing a cat water fountain, there are several factors to consider, including:
1. **Size**: Choose a fountain that is large enough for your cat to comfortably drink from.
2. **Noise level**: If your cat is sensitive to noise, choose a fountain with a quiet pump or a gravity-fed model.
3. **Maintenance requirements**: Choose a fountain that is easy to clean and maintain.

## Common Mistakes to Avoid When Introducing a Cat Water Fountain
Here are some common mistakes to avoid when introducing a cat water fountain to your home:
1. **Placing the fountain in a high-traffic area**: Cats prefer a quiet and private area to drink.
2. **Not providing a large enough water supply**: Make sure the fountain has a large enough water reservoir to meet your cat's needs.
3. **Not cleaning the fountain regularly**: Clean the fountain regularly to prevent bacterial growth and keep your cat healthy.
