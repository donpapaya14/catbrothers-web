## Whisker Stress Science Explained
Whisker fatigue is a common issue in cats, caused by **deep bowls** that trigger barbering and food avoidance behaviors. According to the [Cornell Feline Health Center](https://www.vet.cornell.edu/departments-centers-and-institutes/cornell-feline-health-center), 2022 research found that **71% of cats** experience whisker stress. This stress can lead to decreased appetite and overall health issues.

## When To Choose Alternatives
In certain scenarios, such as **arthritic cats** or **multi-cat homes**, elevated or ceramic bowls may be a better option. According to the [Tufts Cat Behavior Clinic](https://vet.tufts.edu/), 2021, **elevated bowls** can reduce strain on a cat's joints. For multi-cat homes, **ceramic bowls** can help prevent cross-contamination.
