## Tips for Training Your Cats
Training your cats to use an automatic feeder can be a challenging task. According to [The Spruce Pets](https://www.thesprucepets.com), it's essential to **introduce the feeder gradually** and **reward good behavior**. Here are some tips to get you started:
1. Place the feeder in a quiet area where your cats can eat without distractions.
2. Fill the feeder with your cat's favorite food to make it more appealing.
3. Gradually introduce the feeder to your cats over a period of days or weeks.
