## How to Choose the Right Size

Follow the Cat Fanciers' Association [(2021)](https://cfa.org) sizing protocol:
1. Measure your cat from nose to tail base + 2-3" clearance
2. Add 50% to their height when sitting
3. Test carrier space by having your cat turn 360° comfortably

**Example**: A 15" long cat needs at least an 18" carrier. For larger breeds like Maine Coons, opt for 22"+ models like the [EliteField 3-in-1 en Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=EliteField+3-in-1&tag=vds96-20).

## Tips for Acclimating Your Cat

Certified Animal Behaviorist Dr. Ellis [(2022)](https://www.animalbehaviorist.org) suggests this 7-day plan:
1. **Days 1-3**: Place carrier near feeding area with door open
2. **Days 4-5**: Feed meals inside carrier with familiar bedding
3. **Days 6-7**: Practice 5-minute closed-door sessions with treats

**Pro Tip**: Spray Feliway pheromones inside 15 minutes before use to reduce anxiety by 27% ([Journal of Feline Medicine, 2021](https://journals.sagepub.com)).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are soft sided cat carriers airline approved?
Yes, select models like the Sherpa Original meet FAA requirements when dimensions are under 18"x11"x11". Always verify with IATA guidelines ([International Air Transport Association, 2023](https://www.iata.org)).

### How much weight can soft carriers hold?
Most support 15-25 lbs. The Sleepypod Atom holds up to 30 lbs with its aircraft-grade aluminum frame ([Consumer Reports, 2022](https://www.consumerreports.org)).

### Can cats chew through soft carriers?
Polyester mesh resists tearing 3x better than nylon ([Textile Research Journal, 2020](https://www.trj.sagepub.com)). For persistent chewers, apply bitter apple spray to edges.

### Do vets recommend soft or hard carriers?
The AAHA 2022 survey shows 68% of vets prefer soft carriers for routine visits but recommend hard shells for post-surgery transport.

### How to clean urine from a soft carrier?
1. Blot immediately with paper towels
2. Apply enzyme cleaner (e.g., Nature's Miracle)
3. Air dry completely to prevent mildew
