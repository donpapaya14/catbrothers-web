## Introduction to Cat Water Fountains

Cats instinctively prefer **running water** due to their evolutionary history. According to [International Cat Care (2020)](https://icatcare.org), felines are hardwired to seek moving water as stagnant sources in nature often harbor bacteria. A 2020 study found **72% of domestic cats** showed immediate interest in flowing water when introduced to a fountain, compared to still bowls.

## Health Benefits of Running Water for Cats

Proper hydration prevents **feline lower urinary tract disease (FLUTD)**, which affects **1 in 3 cats** according to the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery (2018)](https://journals.sagepub.com). Key benefits:
1. **30-50% increased water intake** vs. bowls (University of Tennessee, 2019)
2. Reduced risk of kidney disease, the **#1 cause of death in senior cats**
3. Prevents dehydration that can lead to constipation

## Tips for Encouraging Cats to Use a Water Fountain

[The Spruce Pets (2022)](https://www.thesprucepets.com) suggests:
1. Place the fountain **at least 3 feet away** from food bowls (cats naturally avoid drinking near food)
2. Use room-temperature water - **60-70°F** is ideal
3. Add ice cubes initially to simulate moving water

## Maintenance and Cleaning of Cat Water Fountains

[PetSafe (2020)](https://www.petsafe.net) cleaning protocol:
1. **Daily**: Rinse reservoir with hot water
2. **Weekly**: Disassemble and soak parts in 1:1 vinegar solution
3. **Monthly**: Replace carbon filters (or per manufacturer guidelines)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do cats really drink more from fountains?
Yes, cats drink **up to 50% more** from running water sources according to University of Tennessee studies. The movement triggers their instinct to seek fresh water.

### How often should I change fountain water?
Replace water completely every **2-3 days** and top off daily. Stagnant water grows biofilm in as little as 48 hours (AVMA, 2021).

### Are plastic fountains safe?
BPA-free plastic is safe, but stainless steel is preferable. A 2020 Tufts University study found **plastic harbors 3x more bacteria** over time.

### What's the quietest cat water fountain?
The Pioneer Pet Raindrop operates at **under 30dB**, quieter than a whisper. Most fountains range between 30-45dB.

### Can I use tap water in cat fountains?
Filtered or distilled water is best to prevent mineral buildup. Hard water leaves deposits that require **weekly descaling with vinegar**.
