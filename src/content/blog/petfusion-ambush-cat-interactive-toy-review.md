### What’s the best interactive toy for aggressive chewers?
The PetFusion Ambush’s **reinforced fabric and metal hinges** withstand heavy chewing. A 2021 [Tufts University study](https://vet.tufts.edu) found durable toys reduce destructive behavior by 40% in high-energy cats.

### How often should I replace the feathers?
Replace feather wands **every 2-3 months** or when visibly frayed. Rotating between 2-3 sets (like [ SmartyKat Skitter Critters en Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=+SmartyKat+Skitter+Critters&tag=vds96-20)) prolongs interest.

### Do lazy cats enjoy interactive toys?
Yes, but start with **5-minute sessions**. A [Cornell Feline Health Center (2019)](https://www.vet.cornell.edu) study showed even sedentary cats increase activity by 70% with gradual introduction.

### Can kittens use the PetFusion Ambush?
Yes, but **supervise play** and use the easiest setting. Kittens under 6 months need shorter, more frequent sessions (3x daily for 10 minutes).

### How do I clean the toy?
Wipe the base weekly with a **damp cloth** and machine-wash feather attachments in a **mesh bag** (cold water, air dry).
