## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best senior cat food for indoor cats?
The best senior cat food for indoor cats is one that is high in protein and moderate in fat, with a balanced mix of vitamins and minerals. **Hill's Science Diet Adult 7+ Indoor Cat Food** is a good option.
### How often should I feed my senior cat?
Senior cats should be fed 2-3 times per day, depending on their individual needs and activity level.
### Can I mix senior cat food with other foods?
It is not recommended to mix senior cat food with other foods, as this can disrupt the balance of nutrients and cause digestive upset.
### What are the common health issues in senior cats?
Common health issues in senior cats include kidney disease, arthritis, and dental problems.
### How can I transition my cat to a new senior cat food?
To transition your cat to a new senior cat food, start by mixing a small amount of the new food with their current food and gradually increase the proportion of new food over 7-10 days.
