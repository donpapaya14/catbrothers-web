## What is the Current Guinness World Record for Longest Duration a Cat Can be Held Upside Down?
The record was set by Robert Dollwet and his cat, Didga, in Australia. Didga is a skilled feline acrobat, known for her **feline agility** and **cat record holder** status. ## How to Teach Your Cat to Enjoy Being Held Upside Down
If you're interested in teaching your cat to enjoy being held upside down, here's a step-by-step guide:
1. Start by gently lifting your cat's front paws off the ground.

3. Reward your cat with treats and praise for their calm behavior.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the average duration a cat can be held upside down?
The average duration a cat can be held upside down is around **10-15 seconds**, according to a study published in the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery (2017)](https://www.sciencedirect.com).
### How often should I hold my cat upside down?
It's recommended to hold your cat upside down no more than **2-3 times a week**, as excessive handling can cause stress and discomfort.
### What are the benefits of holding a cat upside down?
Holding a cat upside down can help strengthen the bond between you and your cat, as well as provide exercise and mental stimulation.
### Can all cat breeds be held upside down?
No, not all cat breeds are suitable for being held upside down. Some breeds, such as **British Shorthairs**, may be too heavy or have a more laid-back temperament, making them less suitable for acrobatic activities.
### How can I make my cat more comfortable when holding them upside down?
You can make your cat more comfortable by supporting their body, providing a secure grip, and monitoring their stress levels.
### What are some signs of stress in cats when held upside down?
Signs of stress in cats when held upside down include **panting**, **struggling**, and **vocalization**. If you notice any of these signs, it's essential to stop immediately and give your cat a break.
