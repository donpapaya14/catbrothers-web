## Where Sensors Fail with Big Paws
Sensors can fail with large cats due to their big paws. According to Engineer interviews from PetSafe, the placement of sensors is critical to prevent interruption errors. Here are some tips for placing sensors:
1. **Place sensors at least 2 inches away** from the entrance of the litter box.
2. **Use a sensor with a large detection area** to reduce the likelihood of interruption errors.
