## Best cat water fountain for small apartments 2024
The best cat water fountain for small apartments is one that is space-saving, quiet, and encourages hydration. According to a study by the [Cornell Feline Health Center](https://www.vet.cornell.edu/departments-centers-and-institutes/cornell-feline-health-center), cats in small homes tend to drink less water due to the lack of running water sources.

## Why small apartments need specialized fountains
Space-saving designs are crucial in small apartments, where every inch counts. Standard fountains can be too large and noisy, which can be a problem in confined spaces. A study by [Consumer Reports](https://www.consumerreports.org/) found that cats prefer running water, but the noise level of standard fountains can be a deterrent. The [Cornell Feline Health Center](https://www.vet.cornell.edu/departments-centers-and-institutes/cornell-feline-health-center) recommends a fountain with a decibel rating below 30dB.

## Top 3 features to prioritize
When choosing a cat water fountain for a small apartment, there are three key features to prioritize:
1. **Dimensions**: Look for a fountain with dimensions under 8x8 inches to save space.
2. **Decibel rating**: A decibel rating below 30dB is essential to minimize noise.
3. **Non-slip base**: A non-slip base is crucial to prevent the fountain from slipping off the counter.

## Pro tips for tiny space placement
When placing a cat water fountain in a small apartment, consider the following tips:
1. **Use vertical space**: Place the fountain on a high shelf or cat tower to save floor space.
2. **Pair with litter box area**: Place the fountain near the litter box to encourage hydration after using the litter box.
3. **Avoid electrical hazards**: Keep the fountain away from electrical outlets and cords to prevent accidents.
