## Introduction to Feline Diabetes
Feline diabetes affects **1 in 200 cats**, with obesity being the leading risk factor (American Animal Hospital Association, 2020). Symptoms include excessive thirst, weight loss despite increased appetite, and lethargy. Diagnosis requires blood glucose test (>250 mg/dL) and urine glucose detection. Early intervention with diet changes can reverse **30-50% of cases** when caught early (AAHA, 2020).

## Nutritional Requirements for Diabetic Cats
Diabetic cats need **high-protein (40-50% of calories), moderate-fat (30-40%), and low-carbohydrate (<10%)** diets (Journal of Veterinary Internal Medicine, 2018). Key nutrients:
- **Animal-based proteins**: Chicken, turkey, or fish support lean muscle mass
- **Omega-3s**: Reduce inflammation linked to insulin resistance
- **Fiber**: 2-8% crude fiber slows glucose absorption

## Benefits of Wet Food for Diabetic Cats
Wet food provides three critical advantages for **feline diabetes management**:
1. **72-78% moisture content** vs. 10% in dry food, reducing dehydration risks (Journal of Animal Science, 2019)
2. **5-10% carbohydrates** compared to 25-50% in kibble
3. **Faster satiety**: 30% fewer calories per gram than dry food

## Transitioning Diabetic Cats to Wet Food
Follow this 14-day protocol (International Journal of Veterinary Medicine, 2020):
1. **Days 1-3**: Replace 25% of current food with wet food
2. **Days 4-7**: Increase to 50% replacement
3. **Days 8-10**: 75% wet food
4. **Blood glucose checks**: Test before meals 2x daily during transition

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can diabetic cats eat dry food at all?
**Dry food can be used sparingly if it contains <10% carbs.** The American Association of Feline Practitioners (2021) recommends limiting kibble to <20% of daily calories for diabetic cats. Look for grain-free formulas with >40% protein.

### How much wet food should a diabetic cat eat daily?
**Feed 20-30 calories per pound of ideal body weight**, divided into 3-4 meals. A 10 lb cat needs 200-300 kcal/day. Use a kitchen scale to measure portions - most 3oz cans contain 70-100 kcal.

### What's the best protein source for diabetic cats?
**Poultry and rabbit are optimal** due to high bioavailability. A Cornell University study (2020) found chicken-based diets improved glycemic control 18% better than fish-based options in diabetic cats.

### How quickly does wet food improve diabetes symptoms?
**Blood glucose typically drops within 7-10 days.** The Journal of Feline Medicine (2019) documented 41% of cats showing normalized water intake within one week of switching to low-carb wet food.

### Should diabetic cats eat prescription or regular wet food?
**Prescription diets show better outcomes.** Veterinary Information Network (2022) data shows prescription wet foods achieve remission in 58% of cases vs. 32% with over-the-counter low-carb foods.
