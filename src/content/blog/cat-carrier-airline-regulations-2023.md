## Airline Carrier Regulations: A Quick Overview
When it comes to traveling with your cat, understanding **cat carrier airline regulations 2023** is crucial for a safe and stress-free journey. According to the [International Air Transport Association (IATA)](https://www.iata.org), major airlines such as American Airlines, Delta, and United have specific regulations regarding cat carrier size and safety. For instance, **American Airlines** requires cat carriers to be no larger than 18.5 x 13.5 x 8.5 inches, while **Delta** allows carriers up to 22 x 14 x 9 inches.

## Measuring Your Cat for the Perfect Carrier Fit
To ensure a comfortable and safe travel experience for your cat, it's essential to measure your cat's length, height, and girth. The [Humane Society's Guide to Traveling with Pets (2020)](https://www.humanesociety.org) provides step-by-step instructions on how to measure your cat:
1. Measure your cat's length from the base of the neck to the base of the tail.
2. Measure your cat's height from the top of the head to the ground.
3. Measure your cat's girth around the widest part of the body.
