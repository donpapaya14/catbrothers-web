The preference for running water is rooted in the cat's wild ancestry, where they would often drink from streams or rivers. ## Tips for Choosing the Right Cat Water Fountain
When selecting a **cat water fountain**, consider the following factors:
1. **Size**: Choose a fountain that is proportional to your cat's size and water needs.
2. **Noise level**: Opt for a fountain with a quiet operation to minimize disturbance.
3. **Maintenance**: Select a fountain with an easy-to-clean design to prevent bacterial growth.
