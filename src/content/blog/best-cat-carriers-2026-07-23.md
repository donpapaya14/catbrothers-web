## Choosing the Right Carrier Size
Selecting the correct **portable cat carrier** size is crucial for your cat's comfort. According to the [American Animal Hospital Association](https://www.aaha.org/), 2020, the carrier should be at least 1.5 times the length of your cat. Here are some guidelines to follow:
1. Measure your cat's length, width, and height.
2. Add 1-2 inches to each dimension for comfort.
3. Choose a carrier that matches these dimensions.

## Carrier Materials and Durability
The material of the **cat carrier** affects its durability and ease of cleaning. A study by the [Journal of Feline Medicine and Surgery](https://www.jfms.com/), 2019, found that **nylon** and **polyester** carriers are more durable than plastic ones. However, plastic carriers are easier to clean.

## Airline Approved Cat Carriers
If you're traveling by air, you'll need an **airline approved cat carrier**. The [Federal Aviation Administration](https://www.faa.gov/) regulates the size and type of carriers allowed on planes. Here are some guidelines:
1. Check with your airline for their specific regulations.
2. Choose a carrier that meets the FAA's size and material requirements.
3. Ensure your cat is comfortable and secure in the carrier.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### What is the best cat carrier for travel?
The best **cat carrier for travel** depends on your cat's size, breed, and personal preferences. According to a study by [University of California, Davis](https://www.ucdavis.edu/), 2020, soft-sided carriers are more comfortable for cats, while hard-sided carriers are more durable.
### How do I choose the right size cat carrier?
Choose a carrier that is at least 1.5 times the length of your cat, as per the [American Animal Hospital Association](https://www.aaha.org/), 2020.
### Can I use a cat carrier for other pets?
No, **cat carriers** are designed specifically for cats, and using them for other pets may not be safe or comfortable.
### How often should I clean my cat carrier?
Clean your **cat carrier** regularly, ideally after each use, to prevent odors and bacteria, as per [Jackson Galaxy](https://www.jacksongalaxy.com/), 2021.
### What are the benefits of using a cat carrier?
Using a **cat carrier** can reduce your cat's stress and anxiety during travel, and also provide a safe and comfortable space for them to rest.
### Can I use a cat carrier for international travel?
Yes, but you'll need to check with your airline and the destination country's regulations regarding **airline approved cat carriers**.
