The benefits of soft sided cat carriers include their ability to be easily stowed away in a car or plane, making them ideal for **collapsible cat carriers** and **portable cat carriers**.

## Choosing the Right Size and Material
When selecting a soft sided cat carrier, it's essential to consider the size and material. According to International Cat Care (2019), **cats prefer carriers with a diameter of at least 1.5 times their body length**. The material should be durable, easy to clean, and provide adequate ventilation. Here are some guidelines for selecting the right size and material:
1. Measure your cat's length and width to determine the minimum carrier size.
2. Choose a carrier with a sturdy material, such as polyester or nylon.
3. Consider the frequency of travel and the level of ventilation required.

## Safety Features to Consider
When it comes to soft sided cat carriers, safety is a top priority. According to the Consumer Product Safety Commission (2020), **ventilation, escape-proof zippers, and reinforced stitching** are essential safety features to look for. Here are some key safety features to consider:
1. Ventilation: Look for carriers with mesh windows or ventilation holes to ensure adequate airflow.
2. Escape-proof zippers: Choose carriers with secure zippers that can't be easily opened by your cat.
3. Reinforced stitching: Select carriers with sturdy stitching that can withstand regular use.
