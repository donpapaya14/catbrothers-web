## Introduction to Sterilized Cat Calorie Calculator
To manage your **sterilized cat's weight**, it's crucial to understand their unique calorie needs. According to the [WALTHAM Centre for Pet Nutrition](https://www.waltham.com/), hormonal changes post-surgery can reduce a cat's metabolic rate by **20-25%** due to leptin resistance. This significant change necessitates a tailored approach to their diet.

## Why Sterilized Cats Gain Weight
The reduction in metabolic rate is not the only factor; fat cells also permanently multiply during puberty, making weight management more challenging post-surgery. As noted by the [WALTHAM Centre for Pet Nutrition, 2018](https://www.waltham.com/), this biological change underscores the importance of monitoring and adjusting your cat's calorie intake.

## The 3-Step Calorie Formula Vets Don't Share
Calculating the exact calorie needs of your sterilized cat involves a **3-step formula**: 
1. Calculate your cat's weight in kilograms to the power of 0.75, then multiply by 70.
2. Apply an **activity factor** (0.8 for indoor cats, 1.2 for outdoor cats).
3. Adjust for neutering by multiplying by 0.8.

| Cat Weight (kg) | Activity Factor | Neutering Adjustment | Total Calories |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 4 | 0.8 (indoor) | 0.8 | (4^0.75 * 70) * 0.8 * 0.8 |
| 4 | 1.2 (outdoor) | 0.8 | (4^0.75 * 70) * 1.2 * 0.8 |

## When to Adjust Calories
Beyond weight changes, there are other signs that your cat may need a calorie adjustment. A **dull coat** may indicate a need for an additional **5% calories**, while changes in litter box habits could signal a need for a hydration check. Seasonal adjustments, especially for winter, may also be necessary. According to the [Tufts University Clinical Nutrition Service, 2020](https://vet.tufts.edu/nutrition/), monitoring these signs can help you make informed decisions about your cat's diet.

## Frequently Asked Questions
### How many calories does a sterilized cat need per day?
The calorie needs of a sterilized cat depend on their weight and activity level. Using the 3-step formula can provide a more accurate estimate.

### What is the best food for a sterilized cat?
The best food for a sterilized cat is one that is balanced and meets their specific calorie and nutritional needs. Consult with your vet to find the most suitable option.

### Can I use a regular feeder for my multi-cat household?
While possible, using a regular feeder can lead to food theft and overeating. Consider microchip feeders or timed dispensers for more controlled feeding.

### How often should I adjust my cat's calorie intake?
Adjust your cat's calorie intake as needed based on signs such as weight changes, dull coat, or changes in litter box habits.

### What role does hydration play in my cat's diet?
Hydration is crucial for your cat's overall health. Ensure your cat always has access to fresh water, and consider a wet food diet for additional moisture.

### How can I prevent my cat from becoming obese?
Preventing obesity in cats involves monitoring their weight, adjusting their calorie intake as needed, and ensuring they get enough physical activity.
