## Introduction to Air Travel with Cats

Flying with cats requires an **airline-approved cat carrier** that meets strict size and material guidelines. According to the [International Air Transport Association (IATA) 2022](https://www.iata.org), carriers must be **ventilated, secure, and fit under the seat** for in-cabin travel. Most airlines allow a maximum size of **18" x 11" x 11"** for in-cabin carriers, while checked baggage options vary by airline. Soft-sided carriers like the [ SturdiBag Pet Carrier en Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=+SturdiBag+Pet+Carrier&tag=vds96-20) are popular for their flexibility and compliance with most airline policies.

## Checked Baggage Cat Carriers

For cats traveling in cargo, the [American Kennel Club (AKC) 2023](https://www.akc.org) recommends **hard-sided crates** like the Petmate Sky Kennel. Key features include:
- **Metal bolts** (not plastic) for security
- **Live animal stickers** for visibility
- **Absorbent bedding** for accidents

Soft-sided crates are **not approved** for cargo travel due to safety risks.

## Airline Approval and Restrictions

Each airline has specific rules for **cat carriers for air travel**:
1. **Delta**: 18" x 11" x 11" max for in-cabin (soft-sided only)
2. **United**: 17.5" x 9" x 10" for in-cabin; hard-sided required for cargo
3. **Southwest**: No pets in cargo; in-cabin carriers must fit under seat

Brachycephalic breeds (e.g., Persians) face restrictions on some airlines due to breathing risks ([FAA 2022](https://www.faa.gov)).

## Tips for Calming Cats During Air Travel

The [American Animal Hospital Association (AAHA) 2023](https://www.aaha.org) recommends:
1. **Acclimate your cat** to the carrier 2-4 weeks pre-flight with short practice sessions.
2. **Use Feliway spray** (pheromone-based) 15 minutes before travel to reduce stress.
3. **Withhold food** 4-6 hours before the flight to prevent nausea.
4. **Line the carrier** with a familiar blanket for scent comfort.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What size cat carrier is allowed on airplanes?
Most airlines allow **18" x 11" x 11"** for in-cabin carriers, but check with your specific airline. For example, JetBlue permits 17" x 12.5" x 8.5".

### Can I sedate my cat for air travel?
No. The [American Veterinary Medical Association (AVMA)](https://www.avma.org) warns that sedation can cause breathing issues at high altitudes. Use pheromone sprays instead.

### How much do airlines charge for cat carriers?
Fees range from **$95-$125 each way** for in-cabin pets. Cargo transport costs **$200-$400**, depending on the airline and route.

### Are emotional support cats allowed on planes?
As of 2021, U.S. airlines no longer recognize emotional support animals as service animals. Only trained service dogs are permitted free of charge.
