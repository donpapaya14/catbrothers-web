## Introduction to Small Space Cat Litter Boxes
When it comes to finding the perfect **small space cat litter box**, it's essential to consider the size and layout of your home. According to the International Association of Animal Behavior Consultants (IAABC, 2018), the minimum litter box size should be 1.5 times the length of your cat. For small spaces, a **compact cat litter box** is ideal.

## Understanding Litter Box Size Requirements
The IAABC recommends that litter boxes should be at least 16 inches long, 12 inches wide, and 6 inches tall. However, for larger cats, a bigger litter box may be necessary. A study by the [University of California, Davis](https://www.ucdavis.edu) found that cats prefer litter boxes with low sides and a non-slip surface.

## Litter Box Placement Strategies for Small Spaces
To minimize odors and maximize ventilation, place the litter box in a well-ventilated area, such as a laundry room or bathroom. According to [Cat Behavior Associates](https://catbehaviorassociates.com), the litter box should be at least 3 feet away from your cat's food and water bowls.

## Cat Litter Box Maintenance for Small Spaces
To maintain a litter box in a small space, scoop the litter box daily, and change the litter completely every 7-10 days. According to [Cat Health](https://cathealth.com), it's essential to use a high-quality litter that can absorb odors and moisture.
