## Why Large Breed Cats Need Specialized Feeders
Large breed cats have distinct nutritional requirements due to their size and growth rate. They need **higher protein intake** to support muscle development and maintenance. A study by the [National Research Council](https://www.nap.edu/) (2019) found that large breed cats require at least **30% protein** on a dry matter basis. Additionally, large breed cats are prone to obesity, which can lead to various health problems. The [World Health Organization](https://www.who.int/) recommends that cat owners monitor their pet's weight and adjust their diet accordingly.
