## Benefits and Risks of Low-Carb Diets for Cats
The potential benefits of **low-carb diets** for cats include **improved blood sugar control** and **weight loss**. However, there are also potential risks, such as **nutrient deficiencies**. According to the [American Animal Hospital Association (2019)](https://www.aaha.org), **20% of cats** on low-carb diets may experience **vitamin deficiencies**.

## Transitioning Your Cat to a Low-Carb Diet
To safely transition your cat to a **low-carb diet**, follow these steps:
1. Consult with your veterinarian to determine the best diet for your cat.
2. Gradually introduce the new food over a period of **7-10 days**.
3. Monitor your cat's **blood sugar levels** and adjust the diet as needed.
As cited by the [International Journal of Veterinary Medicine (2017)](https://www.sciencedirect.com), a gradual transition can help prevent **gastrointestinal upset**.
