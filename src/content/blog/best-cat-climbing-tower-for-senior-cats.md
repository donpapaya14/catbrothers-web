## Tips for Introducing a Cat Climbing Tower to Your Senior Cat
Introducing a **cat climbing tower** to your senior cat can be a great way to provide them with exercise and stimulation. Here are some tips for introducing a cat climbing tower to your senior cat:
1. **Place it in a quiet area**: Place the cat climbing tower in a quiet area of your home where your senior cat can climb and relax without being disturbed.
2. **Encourage use**: Encourage your senior cat to use the cat climbing tower by placing treats or toys on the different levels.
3. **Monitor behavior**: Monitor your senior cat's behavior and adjust the cat climbing tower as needed to ensure their safety and comfort.
